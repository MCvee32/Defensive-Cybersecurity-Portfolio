# Part 1 - A8: Discover Cryptography Used in Internet of Things Devices

### Implementation Discovered
AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)
AES is used in IoT devices for encrypting data

**Used In**
- Smart home devices e.g. Amazon Alexa, Smart lightbulbs
- Smartwatches, fitness trackers

**Purpose**
AES encrypts data transmitted between devices and the cloud, ensuring confidentiality

**Evidence/Example**
The Apple Watch uses AES-256 encryption for secure communication with its paired phone and Apple's iCloud. Data such as health metrics and messages are encrypted both in transit and at rest. This ensures confidentiality and integrity of user information.
