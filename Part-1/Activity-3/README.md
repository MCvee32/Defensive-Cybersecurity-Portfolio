# Part 1 - A3: Discover Security Concepts in Your House

## Security Concepts Discovered

### 1. VPN (Virtual Private Network)
**Location/System** Network / Personal Devices

**How It Works**
- My VPN encrypts internet traffic between my devices and a remote server
- It masks my IP address and location from external networks

**Security Benefits**
- Protects personal data from hackers
- Increases privacy when online

---

### 2. Security Cameras
**Location/System** Entry Points of House

**How It Works**
- Cameras record live video of front and back doors
- Motion detection triggers alerts to my phone
- Recorded footage can be accessed through my phone for monitoring

**Security Benefits**
- Deters thiefs and intruders
- Provides evidence for cases of theft or damage to property
- Allows remote monitoring of my home

---

### 3. Security Alarms

**Location/System** Inside House and Entry Points

**How It Works**
- When activated, sensors detect movement that trigger alarms and notifications sent to phone
- Alerts myself and neighbours of a potential break-in

**Security Benefits**
- Alerts me immediately to potential break-ins
- Deters thiefs and intruders
