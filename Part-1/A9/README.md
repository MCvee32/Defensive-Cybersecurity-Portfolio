# Part 1 - A9: Discover a Privacy Technique Used Online

### Privacy Technique Discovered
End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)
E2EE is a privacy technique used in online communication platforms,like WhatsApp, to ensure that only the communicating users can access and read the message. Protecting privacy of users' chats.

**Used In**
- Messaging platforms/application
- Video conferencing platfroms
- Secure email services

**Purpose**
E2EE protects user privacy by encrypting messages on the sender's device and decrypting them only on the recipent's device. This prevents unauthorised third parties from accessing the message during transmission.
