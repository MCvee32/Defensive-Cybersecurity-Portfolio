### Activity 2: Privacy
- Each member of the group selects a number
- Objective is to get the average of each member's number without ever having to reveal your actual number
- We used multi-party computation:
  - For n group members, each member divides their number into n-shares.
  - Each member keeps a share of their number and recevies a share from each member of the group
  - Each member sums their received shares and their own share
  - Average is calculated from each member's sum of shares
